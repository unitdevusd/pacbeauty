Teresa's Beauty Salon

Teresa's Beauty Salon Hair Salon is a full service men and women’s hair salon located around Stockton’s Country Club and University of the Pacific. Our team of professional, talented, and continuously educated stylists are committed to working with each and every client to achieve the look that is best suited for them.

We strive for the highest quality of service, while maintaining a fun, friendly, non-pretentious environment.
